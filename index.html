Geography


Wikipedia says:

Wales (Welsh: Cymru ['kemri]) is a country that is part of the United Kingdom. It is bordered by England to the east, the Irish Sea to the north and west, and the Bristol Channel to the south. Wales is largely mountainous with its higher peaks in the north and central areas. The country lies within the north temperate zone and has a changeable, maritime climate.
